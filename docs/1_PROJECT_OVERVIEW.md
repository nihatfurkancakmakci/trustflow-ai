# TrustFlow AI - Project Overview

## 1. Executive Summary
TrustFlow AI is an AI-powered Project Operating System serving as a trusted, neutral infrastructure layer between freelancers and clients. By bridging the gap between disconnected workflows (WhatsApp, Notion, GitHub, email) and legally binding agreements, TrustFlow AI serves as the single source of truth for project execution, contract versioning, payment escrow, and dispute resolution.

## 2. The Core Problem
The modern freelance ecosystem is plagued by disputes arising from:
- **Unclear Requirements**: Initial scopes are often vague or undocumented.
- **Scope Creep**: Mid-project changes are casually agreed upon in chats but rarely formalized.
- **Weak Contracts**: Lack of legally and technically binding agreements.
- **Subjective Deliverables**: "Done" means different things to the client and the freelancer.
- **Unsafe Payments**: Clients fear paying upfront; freelancers fear not getting paid.

## 3. The TrustFlow AI Solution
TrustFlow AI replaces the fragmented toolstack with a unified operating system:
- **GitHub for Contracts**: Every contract revision is versioned, immutably hashed, and anchored to the Stellar blockchain.
- **AI-Powered Workflows**: Natural language is converted into structured contracts. AI acts as an objective reviewer of deliverables and a neutral dispute assistant.
- **Smart Escrow**: Funds are locked until predefined conditions are met, protecting both parties.
- **Reputation Engine**: A trust score (0-100) dynamically updates based on project success, on-time delivery, and dispute history.

## 4. Product Vision & Positioning
**What TrustFlow AI IS:**
- Project infrastructure and operating system.
- A trust and verification layer.
- A workflow automation tool for contract and escrow management.

**What TrustFlow AI IS NOT:**
- A marketplace or job board.
- A Fiverr or Upwork clone.
- A communication hub (no chat or video calls).

## 5. Main User Roles
- **Client**: Creates projects, funds escrow, reviews and approves deliveries.
- **Freelancer**: Reviews/negotiates contracts, submits deliverables, receives payment.
- **System AI**: Generates contracts, analyzes deliveries, compiles dispute evidence, recalculates trust scores.

## 6. High-Level Workflow
1. **Initiation**: Client creates a project via natural language. AI structures V1 of the contract.
2. **Negotiation**: Revisions are made (V2, V3, etc.). Each version's hash is stored on Stellar.
3. **Commitment**: Both parties approve. Client funds the escrow.
4. **Execution**: Freelancer works. Scope changes create new hashed contract versions.
5. **Delivery & Review**: Freelancer submits work. AI reviews against the current contract version.
6. **Resolution**: Client approves (funds released) or disputes (AI generates evidence report).
7. **Completion**: Trust scores are updated based on the outcome.
